Work Term Report Summer & Fall 2020

Enrolling at the University of Guelph was the first step I took into fulfilling my dream of being a Software Engineer. I applied to the Coop program for Software Engineering as I heard the experience would be valuable (believe me the experience is life-changing). Before I could catch my breath at University, second year crept up to me. Soon I found myself focussing on getting my assignments done on time and show up for the various coop interviews over Winter 2020. Securing the first coop job is always a struggle but luckily, I found myself with a coop offer from the CCS at the University itself.
