Hello I am Nadeem Howlader. I am a web programming enthusiast working towards getting my hands on all the amazing web technologies out there. I believe with my determination, skill and motivated mindset would help me to achieve my dream of becoming a web-developer. Hence, I work on myself continuously, learn something new every day and continue to grow towards making my vision a reality.

The Beginning

I am Bangladeshi by origin and currently pursuing my dream in Canada. I have spent my childhood in Bangladesh, where I grew up learning about how great computers are. At first, when I was in grade 5, I thought all you could do with a computer was play games, but slowly I learnt that it was more capable than I could comprehend.

Programming did not seem appealing to me at first, but slowly looking into it I had a complete change of heart. The journey began with me trying to implement a custom folder icon to my Windows 7 PC. It was quite satisfying, and I envisioned myself being able to control everything within a computer. I began to work hard to make my vision a reality. Passed my high school with remarkable results and then got accepted to the University of Guelph for studying Software Engineering.

Education

At 2018, I began my bachelors at the University of Guelph, Canada. I am studying Software Engineering(Co-op) under Bachelor of Computing. From my keen interest in Mathematics, I chose to pursue a minor in the subject alongside my degree. It has been three years I been in the University of Guelph and I must say I enjoyed every single day of it and look forward to the 2 years I have left to graduate.

Life In Canada

I came to Canada with the sole aim to get direction and kickstart my career. I have learnt to take up the “good” parts from my work experiences and use them to grow as a person. My time as the University’s IT Help Centre staff allowed me to completely delve into how the corporate work life is and how crucial it is to keep improving in your field of expertise.
